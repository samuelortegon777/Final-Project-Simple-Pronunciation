About Me

My name is Samuel Ortegon, and I have been part of Nuevo Gimnasio Cristiano since fifth grade. I am passionate about programming and sports, and throughout my time at school I have always felt supported. Every teacher has been a great help to me, and I can clearly see how God speaks to me through them and how His presence is reflected in their lives.

My dream is to study at Oral Roberts University in Tulsa, Oklahoma, a Christian university well known for its high academic standards. I decided to create this project because pronunciation in English has always been one of my biggest challenges since I started school. I believe this application will help younger students improve their pronunciation, even if just a little, and support them in their learning journey for the future.
